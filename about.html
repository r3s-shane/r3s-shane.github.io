BLUF: My professional focus is helping cybersecurity leaders build and implement information security programs.

I am the Director of Cyber Risk and CISO Advisory at risk3sixty. My teammate, Christian Hyatt and I started our consulting practice about four year ago. We initially got traction by helping CISOs, CTOs, COOs and other security program leaders help their programs get traction.

Early on, the heart of the challenge we were helping solve for involved helping the organization obtain a certification and dealing with difficult auditors. Over time we great into trusted relationships with our clients where we were asked to solve much bigger problems like helping implement enterprise risk management programs, and performing in-depth security program assessments.

Personally speaking, I like to think of myself as the CISO's right hand man. He/She throws problems my way and I own them for them. I have had the opportunity to be a program leader and a true "Virtual CISO" multiple times. I understand the challenges and stress of being responsible for the success of an entire business function. My goal is the make the leader's life easier, the company better, and to help programs run more effectively and efficiently.
